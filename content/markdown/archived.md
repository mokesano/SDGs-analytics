# Archived Research Profiles

Browse all researcher profiles and journals that have been analyzed on Wizdam AI. This archive provides a comprehensive view of research contributions to Sustainable Development Goals.

## Search Archive

Filter archived profiles by:
- **Researcher Name** - Search by author name
- **ORCID iD** - Direct lookup by ORCID identifier
- **Institution** - Filter by affiliated organization
- **SDG Focus** - Find researchers by their primary SDG contribution
- **Date Range** - See when profiles were last analyzed

---

## Recently Archived Researchers

Profiles sorted by most recently analyzed.

### Profile Cards Include:
- 👤 Researcher name and ORCID iD
- 🏛️ Primary institution
- 📊 Total works analyzed
- 🎯 Top SDG contributions (with confidence scores)
- 📅 Last analysis date
- 🔗 Quick link to full profile

## Archived Journals

Browse journal profiles with Scopus metadata:
- 📰 Journal title and ISSN
- 📈 SJR Impact Factor and Quartile
- 🌍 Country and publisher
- 🏷️ Subject areas mapped to SDGs
- 📊 H-index metrics

---

## Archive Statistics

- **Total Researchers**: Count of unique ORCID profiles in archive
- **Total Works**: Publications classified across all profiles
- **Coverage**: SDGs represented in the archive
- **Last Updated**: Most recent analysis timestamp
