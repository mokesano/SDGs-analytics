# Submit Research for Analysis

Upload your research publications for comprehensive SDG classification. Our AI analyzes full-text content to provide accurate Sustainable Development Goal mapping.

## Submission Options

Choose how you want to submit your research:

---

### 📄 Upload PDF

Submit full-text PDF files for the most accurate analysis.

**Supported Formats:**
- PDF documents (max 50MB)
- Multiple files can be uploaded in one batch

**What Gets Analyzed:**
- Title and abstract
- Full text content
- Keywords and references
- Figures and tables (text extraction)

**Processing Time:** 
- Typically 2-5 minutes per document
- Batch uploads processed sequentially

[**Upload PDF Files**](#upload-form)

---

### 🔗 Submit URL

Provide a link to an online article for automatic extraction and analysis.

**Supported Sources:**
- Publisher websites (Elsevier, Springer, Wiley, etc.)
- Repository links (arXiv, PubMed Central, SSRN)
- Open access platforms
- Institutional repositories

**How It Works:**
1. Enter the article URL
2. Our system extracts main content
3. Removes navigation, ads, and sidebar content
4. Analyzes extracted text for SDG relevance

**Processing Time:**
- Usually under 1 minute
- Some sites may require additional processing

[**Submit URL**](#url-form)

---

### 🆔 Enter DOI

Quick lookup using Digital Object Identifier.

**Format Accepted:**
- Plain DOI: `10.1000/journal.123`
- DOI URL: `https://doi.org/10.1000/journal.123`
- DOI link: `http://dx.doi.org/10.1000/journal.123`

**Data Sources:**
- Crossref metadata API
- OpenAlex enrichment
- Direct publisher APIs

**What You Get:**
- Complete bibliographic metadata
- Abstract and keywords
- Author information
- SDG classification results

[**Analyze DOI**](#doi-form)

After processing, each submission receives:

- **Primary SDG** - Best matching goal with confidence score
- **Secondary SDGs** - Other relevant goals
- **Contributor Type** - Active, Relevant, or Discutor

### Detailed Metrics
- Keyword match scores per SDG
- Semantic similarity scores
- Causal relationship indicators
- Overall impact assessment

### Export Options
- Download detailed report (PDF)
- Export classification data (JSON/CSV)
- Generate citation with SDG tags

## Guidelines for Best Results

### For PDF Uploads
✅ Use searchable PDFs (not scanned images)  
✅ Include complete article text  
✅ Ensure good OCR quality if scanned  
❌ Don't upload supplementary materials alone  

### For URL Submissions
✅ Use direct article page URLs  
✅ Prefer publisher or repository sources  
❌ Avoid database search result pages  
❌ Don't submit PDF download links (use PDF upload instead)

### For DOI Lookups
✅ Verify DOI is valid and registered  
✅ Check DOI resolves correctly first  
❌ Don't submit preprint identifiers as DOIs
