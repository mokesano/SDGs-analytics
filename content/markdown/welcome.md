# Welcome to Wizdam AI - SDG Classification Platform

## Empowering Research for Sustainable Development

Wizdam AI is an advanced artificial intelligence platform designed to analyze and classify academic research according to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

---

## What We Do

### AI-Powered SDG Classification

Our platform uses cutting-edge natural language processing and machine learning to:

- **Analyze Research Publications** - From titles, abstracts, and full-text content
- **Map to SDGs** - Classify works across all 17 Sustainable Development Goals
- **Measure Impact** - Quantify researcher contributions to global sustainability
- **Track Trends** - Monitor evolving research focus over time

### Comprehensive Analysis

For each publication, we provide:

🎯 **Primary SDG Match** - Best-fitting goal with confidence score  
📊 **Multi-SDG Relevance** - Secondary and tertiary SDG associations  
🏷️ **Contributor Classification** - Active Contributor, Relevant Contributor, or Discutor  
📈 **Detailed Metrics** - Keyword scores, semantic similarity, causal relationships

## How It Works

### Step 1: Submit Research
Provide content via:
- **ORCID iD** - Analyze entire researcher profile
- **DOI** - Single publication lookup
- **PDF Upload** - Full-text analysis
- **URL Submission** - Web article extraction

### Step 2: AI Analysis
Our system processes content through:
- **Keyword Matching** - SDG-specific vocabulary and targets
- **Semantic Similarity** - Contextual understanding via transformer models
- **Causal Analysis** - Identifying SDG-related mechanisms and outcomes
- **Cross-Reference Enrichment** - Crossref, OpenAlex metadata integration

### Step 3: Get Results
Receive comprehensive classification:
- Interactive SDG dashboard
- Detailed metrics and visualizations
- Downloadable reports (PDF, JSON, CSV)
- Persistent profile URLs for sharing

## About the SDGs

The **Sustainable Development Goals** are 17 interconnected global goals adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015 as part of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

### The 17 SDGs:

1. 🏛️ **No Poverty** - End poverty in all forms everywhere
2. 🌾 **Zero Hunger** - End hunger, achieve food security, promote sustainable agriculture
3. 🏥 **Good Health** - Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all
4. 📚 **Quality Education** - Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education
5. ⚖️ **Gender Equality** - Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls
6. 💧 **Clean Water** - Ensure availability and sustainable management of water
7. ⚡ **Clean Energy** - Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable energy
8. 💼 **Decent Work** - Promote sustained, inclusive economic growth
9. 🔬 **Innovation** - Build resilient infrastructure, foster innovation
10. 🤝 **Reduced Inequalities** - Reduce inequality within and among countries
11. 🏙️ **Sustainable Cities** - Make cities inclusive, safe, resilient, sustainable
12. ♻️ **Responsible Consumption** - Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns
13. 🌍 **Climate Action** - Take urgent action to combat climate change
14. 🐟 **Life Below Water** - Conserve and sustainably use oceans and marine resources
15. 🌳 **Life on Land** - Protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems
16. ⚖️ **Peace & Justice** - Promote peaceful and inclusive societies
17. 🤝 **Partnerships** - Strengthen means of implementation, revitalize partnerships
