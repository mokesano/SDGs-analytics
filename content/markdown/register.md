# Create Your Account

Join Wizdam AI to access powerful SDG research analysis tools and track your contributions to sustainable development.

## Registration Benefits

### For Researchers
- **Profile Analytics** - Track your SDG contributions across all 17 goals
- **Work Classification** - Automatically classify your publications by SDG relevance
- **Impact Metrics** - See your contributor type: Active Contributor, Relevant Contributor, or Discutor
- **Export Reports** - Download detailed analysis reports for grants and tenure

### For Institutions
- **Team Dashboard** - Monitor collective SDG impact across departments
- **Leaderboard Rankings** - Compare performance with other institutions
- **API Access** - Integrate SDG classification into your systems
