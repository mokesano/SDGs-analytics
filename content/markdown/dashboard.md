# User Dashboard

Welcome to your personal Wizdam AI dashboard. Manage your research analysis, view insights, and track your SDG contributions.

## Quick Stats

Your research impact at a glance.

### This Month
- **Profiles Analyzed**: Track ORCID profiles you've searched
- **Works Classified**: Total publications analyzed for SDG relevance
- **Top SDG**: Your primary area of contribution
- **Submission Queue**: Pending PDF/URL analyses

---

## Recent Activity

### Latest Searches
Your most recent ORCID and journal profile lookups.

### Submissions History
Track the status of your PDF and URL submissions:
- 🟡 Pending - Waiting in queue
- 🔵 Processing - Currently being analyzed  
- 🟢 Complete - Analysis finished
- 🔴 Error - Processing failed

---

## Quick Actions

- **Analyze ORCID** - Search a researcher's profile
- **Check Journal** - Look up Scopus journal metrics
- **Upload PDF** - Submit full-text for deeper analysis
- **Submit URL** - Analyze article from web link

Organize your research into collections for easy reference and reporting.
