# Analytics Dashboard

Comprehensive insights into SDG research trends, institutional impact, and global contribution patterns. Visualize the state of sustainable development research.

## Overview Metrics

### Global Statistics
- **Total Researchers Analyzed**: Unique ORCID profiles in database
- **Total Publications Classified**: Works mapped to SDGs
- **Coverage by SDG**: Distribution across all 17 goals
- **Geographic Reach**: Countries and institutions represented
