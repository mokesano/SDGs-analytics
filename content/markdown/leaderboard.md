## Ranking Categories

### Active Contributors
Researchers whose work has **high confidence scores** (>0.7) and significant SDG-focused content. These are the leading voices in each goal area.

### Relevant Contributors  
Researchers with **moderate confidence scores** (0.4-0.7) whose work relates to the SDG but may not be the primary focus.

### Discutors
Researchers whose work **mentions or discusses** the SDG (**confidence <0.4**) but is not primarily focused on it. Still valuable for interdisciplinary perspectives!

## Leaderboard Metrics

Each researcher profile displays:
- 📊 **Contribution Score** - Based on number of works × average confidence
- 🎯 **Top SDGs** - Primary areas of contribution
- 📈 **Trend** - Whether their SDG focus is increasing or decreasing
- 🔗 **ORCID Profile** - Direct link to full analysis
